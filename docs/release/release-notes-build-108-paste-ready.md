## 🇺🇸 English

Letter Go closes the loop — pick it up, use the code, mark it used.
This release lands the final piece of the promo/coupon hunt and cleans
up levels, the popup, and onboarding into one coherent experience.

**🎟 Coupon redemption loop**
- Brands write the redemption code, link, or store instructions right
  in the compose screen (up to 200 chars)
- Receivers see it as a teal card inside the letter — long-press to
  copy the code
- After redeeming, tap "🎫 Mark as used" and the card fades to gray
  with a strikethrough and a ✓ "Used" badge that persists on re-open

**🏆 Levels now mean real gain**
- Pickup radius grows +10m per level (Free 200m → 690m at Lv 50,
  Premium 1km → 1,490m at Lv 50)
- After Lv 50, earn 🪙 points to spend on your subscription
- Profile 🏆 button opens all 10 tier milestones, from apprentice
  postman up to 👑 the Legendary Letter-Carrier

**🎁 Coupon ticket popup**
- After login, an actual ticket-shaped modal introduces today's brand
  promo
- Side V-notches, dashed divider, 🎟 LIMITED OFFER ribbon, live expiry
  timer
- One appearance per session — close it and it stays closed until the
  next cold start

**🔕 Brand mute**
- Silence a specific brand — future letters stop showing up, past
  coupons stay usable
- Toggle anytime from the letter-read screen's 🔕 icon

**✏️ Compose is dramatically faster**
- Letter body sits right under the destination — no scrolling past
  config to start writing
- 10+ options collapsed into one "More options" panel
- Paper gains a soft gold glow when focused

**🧭 Onboarding rewritten**
- "Pick up discount and promo letters" / "Instantly usable rewards"
  reset the first impression
- 5-tab nav (Explore · Collection · Send · Tower · Profile) is still
  the spine

**🧹 Cleanup & localization**
- Inbox title unified as "Collection", two tabs only (Received · Sent)
- 4-chip filter bar (All · Coupon · Voucher · Brand)
- 11 previously hardcoded Korean strings now localized
- The chain rule (send 3 to read next) is fully gone

Every new string audited across 14 languages (한국어 · English · 日本語 ·
中文 · Français · Deutsch · Español · Português · Русский · Türkçe ·
العربية · Italiano · हिन्दी · ไทย).

Pick up, write, redeem on the spot — Letter Go.
